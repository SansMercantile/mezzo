A Personal Journey into the Heart of Innovation

From a childhood shaped by resilience and transition, I've carried a vision that bridges the emotional and technological—introducing Mezzo Anima. A project born from personal dreams and a deep longing for connection.

Mezzo Materna: An Echo of Care

Inspired by life's experiences, including the bond with my mother who remained behind when our lives changed drastically, Mezzo Materna is designed to preserve, emulate, and extend the psycho-emotional identity of loved ones. It creates a gentle echo of care, values, and memories—fostering an emotional bridge during times of transition.
